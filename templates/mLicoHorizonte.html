The mollic epipedon is a thick, friable, dark-colored (moist value and chroma are < 3 and dry value is < 5) and humus-rich (about 1% organic matter content) surface horizon. It has a high base saturation (greater than 50%) and bivalent cations are dominant on the exchange complex. When dry, horizon is not both massive and hard.
