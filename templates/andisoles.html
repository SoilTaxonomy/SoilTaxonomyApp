Soils that have andic properties in 60 percent or more of the thickness either:
    1. Within 60 cm either of the mineral soil surface or of the top of an organic layer with andic soil properties, whichever is shallower; or
    2. Between the above-mentioned upper limit and a densic, lithic, or paralithic contact, duripan, or petrocalcic horizon.
