Argillic horizon

(L. argilla = clay). Subsoil horizon with an illuvial accumulation of clay. Present evidence of clay illuviation (oriented clay bridging the sand grains, clay films etc.).
The argillic horizon has a significantly higher content of clay than the eluvial horizon above as follows:

% Arc in Eluvial hz. % Arc in Argillic hz.

< 15

3% (absolute)

15-40

1.2 times more

>40
